## The quiet side of the Bukit

Uluwatu draws surfers, honeymooners and anyone tired of Canggu's pace — cliffs,
reef breaks, and a slower rhythm. Thomas Beach sits below the estate: a short,
walkable stretch of sand rather than a destination beach club.

## Getting here and around

The Bukit is spread out, and most guests want a scooter or a driver — we can
arrange either. Airport transfer is included with a direct booking; onward
trips to Padang Padang, Bingin or Uluwatu Temple are a short ride away.

## Good to know

Construction continues on parts of the estate and nearby, typically 8am–5pm,
and Uluwatu's nightlife carries after dark on some streets. We'd rather tell
you plainly than have the first you hear of it be a review — ask us and we'll
point you to the quietest villa for your dates.
