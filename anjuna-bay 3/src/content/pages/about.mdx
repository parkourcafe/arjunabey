## The estate

Anjuna Bay sits above Thomas Beach on the quiet side of the Bukit peninsula —
cliffside pool villas built in phases, with the completed clusters open for
stays while the rest of the estate continues to take shape nearby.

## The team

The on-the-ground team is the reason guests keep rating their stay so highly.
A named host sends a welcome message the day before arrival, and the response
has earned a reputation for being fast and genuinely helpful — 24 hours a day.
We're changing how the villas are sold and priced; we're not changing the
people who look after you while you're here.

## Honesty, including about construction

Part of the estate is still being built. We'd rather tell you that plainly,
on the villa page, before you book — including which villas are quietest for
your dates — than have you find out from a review after the fact.
