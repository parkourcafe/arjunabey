The breaks that put Uluwatu on the map are a short walk or a quick ride from
the villas. We keep board storage, rinse and a place to leave your things,
because the best session is the one you don't have to plan around.

Padang Padang, Bingin and Impossibles are all within a short drive; Thomas
Beach itself is on your doorstep. Stay seven nights or more on a surf stay and
the nightly rate steps down — this is a length-of-stay rate, not a discount on
the room.
