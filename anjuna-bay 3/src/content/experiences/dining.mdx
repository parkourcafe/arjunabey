Breakfast can be arranged to float in the pool, or laid out quietly on the
terrace — whichever suits the morning. For longer stays or a special evening,
a private chef can be arranged in the villa. Beyond that, the clifftop cafés
— Suka Espresso, Drifter, the Cashew Tree — are all a short walk or ride away.
