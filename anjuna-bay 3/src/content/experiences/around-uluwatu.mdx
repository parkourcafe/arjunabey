Uluwatu is spread out, and the honest answer is: you'll want a scooter or a
driver. We can arrange either for you, and every villa page tells you exactly
how far the walk to Thomas Beach really is.

Uluwatu Temple and the Kecak fire dance at sunset are worth the trip. Beyond
Thomas Beach, Padang Padang, Bingin, Suluban and Balangan are all within a
short ride — each with its own character and its own tide-dependent access,
so it's worth checking timing before you go.
