Uluwatu tends to come at the end of a Bali trip — the wind-down after Canggu
and Ubud, and often the last stop before the airport. For a honeymoon, that
sequencing works in your favour: arrive tired of crowds, and Uluwatu's cliffs
and quieter pace are exactly the contrast you want.

The three things worth prioritising, in order: a private pool (not a shared
one), an uninterrupted view, and genuine walking distance to a beach worth
walking to. Everything else is negotiable.
