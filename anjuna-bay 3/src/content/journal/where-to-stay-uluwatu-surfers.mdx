Most of Uluwatu's listings are further from the water than their photos
suggest — the cliffs mean a short drive often stands in for what looks, in a
gallery shot, like a walk. If the plan is dawn sessions, the walk-time to the
beach matters more than almost anything else on the listing.

A few things worth checking before you book anywhere on the Bukit: the actual
walk-time to the beach (not the drive-time), whether board storage and rinse
space exist, and whether the access is tide-dependent — several of the best
breaks near Thomas Beach and Padang Padang have stairs that are easier at some
tides than others.
